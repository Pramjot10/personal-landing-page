About Me

Hi, my name is Pramjot Singh. After completing my flight training and earning my pilot's license, I was excited to pursue a career in aviation. However, as I began working as a student pilot, I realized that it wasn't fulfilling my career aspirations as I thought it would. The long hours, the routine nature of the job, and the limited opportunities for growth left me feeling unfulfilled. I had always been interested in technology and decided to take a course in web development to expand my knowledge. As I delved deeper into the world of coding, I discovered that I had a passion for creating beautiful and functional websites. I realized that I could use my skills in web development to make a real impact in the world, and ultimately decided to pursue it as a career. Although it was a difficult decision to leave my dream of becoming a pilot behind, I knew that pursuing a career in web development would offer me more opportunities for growth and fulfillment.
